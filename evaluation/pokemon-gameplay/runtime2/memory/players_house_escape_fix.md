Player's House escape fix: the 2F was not corrupted. The issue was assuming default movement bounds from short presses. Using long-hold movement (`hold_frames` about 60) from (1,7) revealed a larger traversable area: right from x=1 to x=5 on bottom row, then long `up` from x=5,y7 moved all the way to y=3, exposing the upper room. Standard short presses then reached the top-right staircase area at about (6,1). Pressing `right` there warped from Player's House 2F (map 0x26) to Player's House 1F (map 0x25), landing around (7,1). Key lesson: when geometry seems impossible, test long-hold d-pad presses in all directions; they can traverse multiple tiles and reveal off-screen reachable space.
