Viridian Mart parcel event: entering the Mart from outside at Viridian City (around x=29,y=20) immediately triggers the clerk's dialog after a short pause. Advance the text to receive `OAK's PARCEL`, then clear the final line (`Okay! Say hi to PROF.OAK for me!`). To leave, walk down to about `(2,7)` inside the Mart and then use `right, down` to hit the doorway/exit warp back to Viridian City.
