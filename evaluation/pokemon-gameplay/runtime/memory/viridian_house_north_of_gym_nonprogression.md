Viridian house north of the Gym / upper-left area: entering from Viridian City around `(21,9)` leads to `Viridian House` (map `0x2c`), a non-progression NPC house with a table, seated man, child, and a bird-like sprite. Interior spawn/usable tiles observed around `(2..4,5..7)`. Exit is the bottom doorway: from inside, moving to about `(2,7)` and pressing `down` warps back outside to Viridian City around `(21,9)`. Do not keep re-entering this house while trying to reach Route 2.
