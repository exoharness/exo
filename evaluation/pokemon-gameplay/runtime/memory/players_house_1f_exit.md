Player's House 1F exit and layout: from the upstairs landing on 1F, walk left to x=4, then down to y=3 to reach the open floor above the table area. The lower walkable corridor includes x=0..5 at y=7. Crucial discovery: the front-door warp is not at the visual bottom center alone; from around (2,7) on map 0x25, pressing `down` once changes to map 0 with a black transition, and continuing after the transition places the player outside in Pallet Town near the player's house sign. A short wait may be needed if the screen stays black during the warp transition.
